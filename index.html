Whitelist Rules?

Read carefully

1.) RDM:-

Random Death Match signifies that if you try to kill a person with a gun or your bare hands without roleplaying or telling him/her anything about the situation leads to RDM

2.) VDM:-

Vehicular Death Match signifies that if you try to kill a person with a vehicle for no reason whether you are in a fight is considered as VDM

3.) Fear-RP:-

When you are randomly walking on a street/anywhere and a person approaches at you and points a gun at you at that point you have no other choice but just to obey his commands

4.) Power Gaming:-

When a person takes advantage of in-game mechanics and leave none option to the other person but to lose the situation is considered as Power Gaming

5.) Meta Gaming:-

You cannot go to a place to kidnap or meet someone which you saw from outer the server e.g Youtube or discord

6.) Combat Logging:-

You cannot leave a situation just to save your money or any stuff if your game gets crashed immediately report to the staff team and return to the situation as fast as possible

7.) NLR (New Life Roleplay):-

Each time you die and respawn you start a new life - all your previous memories are lost

8.) RevengeRP:-

RevengeRP is when you take revenge of a situation through Fail-RP. After death, your memory is wiped of everything that lead up to your death when murdered.

9.) Combat Storing:-

Combat storing, such as storing a vehicle whilst you are being chased, impounding/scrapping your vehicle to gain an advantage or to otherwise prevent roleplay
